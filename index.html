Welcome to Bridges of the World

According to Wikipedia a bridge is a structure built to span physical obstacles for the purpose of providing passage over the obstacle, without closing or hindering the physical obstacle. Designs of bridges vary depending on the following:

  • the function of the bridge
  • the nature of the terrain where the bridge is constructed and anchored
  • the material used to make it
  • the funds available to build it

Types of Bridges

  1. Beam bridges
  2. Truss bridges
  3. Cantilever bridge
  4. Arch bridge
  5. Tied arch bridge
  6. Suspension bridge
  7. Cable-stayed bridge
