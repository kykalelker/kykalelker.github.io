Welcome to Bridges of the World

According to Wikipedia a bridge is a structure built to span physical obstacles for the purpose of providing passage over the obstacle, without closing or hindering the physical obstacle. Designs of bridges vary depending on the following:

  • the function of the bridge
  • the nature of the terrain where the bridge is constructed and anchored
  • the material used to make it
  • the funds available to build it

Types of Bridges

  1. Beam bridges
  2. Truss bridges
  3. Cantilever bridge
  4. Arch bridge
  5. Tied arch bridge
  6. Suspension bridge
  7. Cable-stayed bridge

Examples of Bridges

  1. Golden Gate Bridge

    The Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco is a suspension bridge

  2. Brooklyn Bridge

    The Brooklyn Bridge in New York City is a cable-stayed bridge

  3. Bandra Worli Sealink

    The Bandra Worli Sealink in Mumbai is another cable-stayed bridge

  4. Coronation Bridge

    The Coronation Bridge in Siliguri is an arch bridge

  5. Slussenomradet Bridges

    The Slussenomradet Bridges in Stockholm are a group of beam bridges
